## Project Steps  

1. **Data Understanding**
   - Reviewed dataset structure and business context  
   - Identified missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies  

2. **Data Cleaning**
   - Removed duplicate records  
   - Standardized salary, date, and numeric fields  
   - Ensured data consistency across departments  

3. **Data Analysis**
   - Calculated total employee count and department-level distribution  
   - Analyzed total and average salary by department  
   - Identified highest and lowest salaries  
   - Evaluated employee performance scores  
   - Analyzed employee tenure and retention trends  

4. **Business Questions Addressed**
   - Total number of employees  
   - Employee count per department  
   - Total and average salary across the organization  
   - Highest and lowest salary  
   - Number of employees with performance scores above 80  
   - Average employee tenure  
   - Department with the longest average tenure  

---

## Results & Insights  
- Clear visibility into workforce size and departmental structure  
- Identification of salary distribution and compensation patterns  
- Recognition of high-performing employees  
- Insights into employee tenure to support retention planning  

These insights demonstrate how Excel can be used to support **data-driven HR and business decisions**.
